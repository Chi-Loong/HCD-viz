Final Projects 2024

Correlation of team's compositions to design project grades (Hendra)

Visualising different factors that may affect teams' performance in a design project. Gender, personality types, pre-university education.

Dog Breed Recommendations with Visual Insights (Camille, Jasmine)

A tool helps users find the top 10 dog breeds that closely match user's preferences. Afterwards, these breeds are displayed in a graph, offering a clear comparison of how they align with user's selected criteria to help them make an informed decision.

Charting Design: A Data-Driven Snapshot of Asia's Design Industry in 2024 (Amrutha, Karis)

This visualisation project analyses the 2024 design industry landscape in Asia, focusing on salary transparency and working conditions. Using data from Design Pay Asia's survey, we uncover insights about the demographics of current designers, compensation patterns, and industry practices to help emerging designers make informed career decisions.

Dashboard to display on hospital improvement ideas by div/years (Sam)

Explore and visualize trends in idea submissions from 2013 to 2024, uncovering insights into organizational innovation patterns and efficiency. The analysis focuses on understanding the dynamics of idea generation, by department/div, type of ideas deposited and implementated within the organization.

Survey on the Historical Output of Characteristic Agricultural Products in Yunnan Province (Shaoying, Liaoliao)

Data visualisation was used to present, investigate and summarise the annual production of three types of speciality agricultural products, namely coffee, fresh cut flowers and mushrooms, as well as their distribution areas in Yunnan Province, China, for the time period approximately between 2016 and 2022.
